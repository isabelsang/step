Background

I was born and raised in New Jersey and am currently working from home in NJ. Jersey gets a pretty bad rap sometimes--people have told me it is the "Armpit of America" or "the ugly stepchild of New York"-- but I love it here. We have everything you could want in a state: a nice beach to relax on, beautiful mountains to go hiking on, NYC (coolest city in the US) within driving distance, and the greatest bagels on the planet.

I am very passionate about the superiority of NJ bagels. You may be skeptical and think that "all bagels taste the same" or that "there's no way that JERSEY of all places makes the best bagels," but let me be the first to tell you that no, not all bagels are created equal and that yes, New Jersey is home to the best bagels.

Don't believe me? You simply HAVE to come to New Jersey and try them. It should be the number one location on your bucket list.
